What is insurance verification?

Insurance verification is the process of confirming a patients eligibility and benefits offered by their insurance company before he or she comes to the appointment. This can help healthcare providers to know how to offer a treatment plan to their clients, having a more precise information about how much they will be expected to pay out-of-pocket. It can also help insured people to know what is covered under their plan, what sometimes can prompt them to take better care of their health.
